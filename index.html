A brief introduction about me


I am Jayanth Kumar. I live in Madurai, also called the Athens of the East owing to its several places of culture that are centuries old. I did my schooling at Vikaasa School from 2009 to 2021, passing out with a score of 89% in grade 12. Ever since my childhood, I've been fond of learning new things, especially electronics. Remember those old times when we actually had toys to play with rather than mobile phones with Talking Tom for kids these days? I usually had the practice of breaking toys apart to explore what's beneath the curtains out of curiosity. As years went by, I began to acquire technical knowledge about almost everything I see and feel at home. I would pester my parents during work and demand an explanation of how an electrical appliance, say a television or a washing machine works.

Back in 2009, my dad bought his first desktop computer. To be precise, the setup consisted of a CRT Monitor (pretty much old-school tech), paired with a CPU which had a ASUS Motherboard equipped with an Intel Pentium Dual Core Processor which had a base clock of 3.1 GHz. The computer was running Windows XP back then. That was the first time when I got an exposure of how a computer actually looked like. I used to scribble some random art in Microsoft Paint, play Minesweeper and used to type documents with random, mostly gibberish on Microsoft Word. As time passed by, I grew up with all the latest trends in tech surrounding me. In fact, my generation (GenZ) were the first to witness a rapid change in technology in just a decade. We saw the transition from feature phones to touch phones which became smart as new updates rolled in. This was a first of its kind feature that redefined the way mobile phones work. Smart phones are already smart and will only get smarter through updates.

Now, back to the present, I'm on my path to become a successful technologist by creating innovative solutions that is easily accessible by everyone around the world and affordable. I have my areas of interest in Web Development, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I love creating projects that boost my creative and technical knowledge, giving me a satisfaction to justify the time spent in creating such projects.

I have a idealistic vision for the future where the Metaverse becomes the new medium for human communication. With the latest innovations in tech such as Blockchain and Extended Reality (XR) are paving way to a new future as one could have never thought of in the past decade. I firmly believe that this new phase of transition will usher a new range of infinite possibilities, all to cater the needs of humanity in its fullest potential. Per Aspera Ad Astra ["Through hardships to the Stars"].
